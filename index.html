About

TechnoMIT-2K18 is an intercollege Tech-Fest conducted by the "CSE" department of Maharashtra Institute of Technology, Aurangabad. It will be a concoction of technical and cultural extravaganza!
TechnoMIT-2K18 will be a fun-filled and exciting inter collegiate festival highlighted by events such as IPL Auction, Treasure Hunt, Gaming, etc. All the events will help the students to propagate their skills and talent.
