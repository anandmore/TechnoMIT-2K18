Structure :

Each group will be assigned a number according to which they will present their poster. There will be only one round according to which Three Winners will be selected. For this Event we will require classrooms (only resource, help of decorative committee) according to entries.

Rules :

  • All the participants must bring their College Id Card.
  • Participants must report half hour before the event start.
  • Participants must bring receipt of the event for verification.
  • Participants should maintain discipline while Attending the event.
  • The decision of Judges will be final.
  • Maximum 2 participants in a team.
  • Size of poster will be A2.
  • Title is short and draws interest.
  • Text should be clear and readable.
  • Participants can use graphics, colors , images , newspaper articles , diagrams etc.
  • The participants name, college name ,department should be positioned at top right corner.
  • If necessary, panels can be added.
  • The poster does not necessarily have to fill the entire working area .
  • The text is clear and to the point use of bullets ,numbering and headlines make it easy to read.
  • The poster must be oriented in a “landscape” position.
  • Result will be based on participants presentation as well as contents of the poster.
  • Time Limit- 5-10 Min
