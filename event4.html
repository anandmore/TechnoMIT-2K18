IPL Auction

  • Objective

  • Team and Fees Structure

Objective :

An event for everyone who not just loves the game of cricket, but analyses it to the limit. This event makes you don the hat of a bidder. You manage your budget and you strategize to make the best possible team competing against 9 opponents who will give you a run for your money, quite literally. If you love cricket, if you have followed IPL and if you can mix common sense with passion into your bidding strategy, this event is necessary for you.

Round 1 : Online Quiz - 20 minutes duration

  • The quiz will contain questions only from Cricket. It will be an elimination round.
  • It will be an IPL QUIZ ROUND having 20-25 Mcqs. 5 questions will be star marked that will be considered in case of a tie between two teams.
  • Top 10 teams clearing the quiz will qualify for the next round. Those 10 teams will be provided with a same amount of capital and according to their positions in the quiz, they will be provided with an additional amount, to give a certain advantage to the teams securing top positions.

Round 2: On-Campus Round - 3-4 hours duration

  • The final teams will compete in a live auction of players. Each team will be given a fixed budget, which will be used for the auction. The aim will be to build a squad that maximizes the total attribute score of the team.

Rules :

  • All the participants must bring their College Id Card.
  • Participants must report half hour before the event start.
  • Participants must bring receipt of the event for verification.
  • Participants should maintain discipline while Attending the event.
  • The decision of Judges will be final.
  • A team must comprise of 2-3 members.
  • Team members must be of same college.
  • No participant can be a part of more than one team.
  • A team member cannot register in more than one team.
  • In case of discrepancies, decision of the judges/event organizers will be final.
  • Teams, which do not adhere to the rules and regulations, can be disqualified at any phase of the event.
  • Organizers reserve the right to change the Timelines.
