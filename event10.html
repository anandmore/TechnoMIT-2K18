Objective :

The main objective of Project Competition is to contribute towards the Technology and Innovation. Describing students Software Development skills, Presentation skill, Technical skills, Communication skills. Best projects will be rewarded based on the Judges judgment.

Structure :

Rules :

  • All the participants must bring their College Id Card.
  • Participants must report half hour before the event start.
  • Participants must bring receipt of the event for verification.
  • Participants should maintain discipline while Attending the event.
  • Any institute/college can send any number of entries.
  • Project can be in form of working model/software/simulation based/industry sponsored/experiments.
  • The project should be implemented on Laptop.
  • Whatever software or setup required should be arranged by own participants.
  • Maximum two members can participate in one project.
  • All the participants present at the exhibition for all days will get a participation certificate.
  • Basic requirement i.e., electric power supply will be made available to participants as per requirement of project.
  • The panel of judges consists of subject experts from various HEIs in Muscat, Industry representatives and CSI staff.
  • The organizers reserve the rights to change any or all of the above rules as they deem fit.
  • Any change in the rules, will be notified to the registered participants.
