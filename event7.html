Dual Extempore

  • Objective
  • Structure
  • Rules
  • Team and Fees Structure

Objective :

To develop a Spontaneity i.e. think on the spot and express your thoughts. It channelizes the thought process. To strengthen students ability in speaking skills as well as their oral interactive strategies.

Round 1 : Dual Extempore

  • In this round two participants will be given same topic picked up by the judges by draw of lots on which they have to speak for 2 minutes.
  • This round consists of non-factual topics.
  • Among two one will proceed to next round.

Round 2 : Faculty Speaking

  • Each participant will be given 1 minute to think and 2 minutes to speak on a topic picked up by him/her by draw of lots.
  • This round consists of factual topics.
  • Participant with highest score will be declared as winner.

Rules :

  • All the participants must bring their College Id Card.
  • Participants must report half hour before the event start.
  • Participants must bring receipt of the event for verification.
  • Participants should maintain discipline while Attending the event.
  • The decision of Judges will be final.
  • Each participant shall be given two minutes to speak on a topic picked up by him/her by draw of lots.
  • A bell will ring at the end of one minute to indicate that one minute is left to finish his/her speech.
  • Another bell will ring at the end of two minutes.
  • Marks will be deducted if the speaker exceeds the time limit.
  • Language used shall be strictly English.
  • All the participants must bring their college ID card.
  • Participants must report half an hour before the event starts.
  • Participants must bring the receipt of the event for verification.
  • No objection shall be entertained as the decision of judges shall be final.

Factual topics (Only For Idea) -

  • Demonetization
  • Freedom of expression
  • The status of women, etc.

Non-Factual topics (Only For Idea) -

  • Failure is a stepping stone to success
  • There is life after death
  • Fragrance, etc.
