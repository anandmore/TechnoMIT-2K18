Objective :

The flexibility in terms of the chosen clues, locations and prizes of the event means that the 'message' or objective of a treasure hunt event can conveyed to teams in engaging fashion.

We have to provide cheat to the participant in which clues are in the form of puzzle.

  • The first cheat is given in class after solving it the participant get the next clue.
  • So they search the place and get the next cheat. In that cheat they find next clue.
  • So again they have to search the place. After getting that cheat there is some task which they have to perform.
  • After completion of that task they have to return back to the start point, then there task is completed.

Puzzle --> Puzzle -->Task

Rules :

  • All the participants must bring their College Id Card.
  • Participants must report half hour before the event start.
  • Participants must bring receipt of the event for verification.
  • Participants should maintain discipline while Attending the event.
  • The decision of Judges will be final.
  • Players can play this game in group of 4.
  • Player can use mobile (only for task of related).
  • Each group allot maximum 20 minute.
  • If you think you are not able to solve the puzzle. You can change the question but four minutes is added to your account.
  • Your group must have specific name.
  • The cheat has number on it you have to carry only those cheat the number you got. If you carry cheat of other number your team was disqualified.
