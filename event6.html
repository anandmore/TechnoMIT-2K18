Hunky Brain

Event Objective :

  • To find the entertainment guru.
  • Test the accumulation and the retention of knowledge of the participants.
  • Bring the like minded individuals at the same stage and create a competitive yet learning environment.

Event Structure :

Main areas of the quiz

  • TV series - GoT, Friends, How I met your mother, Stranger things.
  • Anime - Naruto, DBZ, Bleach, Gintama.
  • Marvel universe - Anything about marvel universe.

Round 1 : MCQ

  • This round is a paper quiz round.

Round 2:

  • This round has two parts -
    • Clip Round
    • Picture/symbol Recognition.

Round 3:

  • This round is a buzzer round which will be the last round of the entire Quiz.

Rules :

  • All the participants must bring their College Id Card.
  • Participants must report half hour before the event start.
  • Participants must bring receipt of the event for verification.
  • Participants should maintain discipline while Attending the event.
  • The decision of Judges will be final.
  • Players can play this game solo or in group of 2.
  • Use of mobile is strictly prohibited. If found, player will be eliminated.
  • Player can choose any main topic or they can choose any one of the main topic from the quiz. E.g. Only TV series
  • Each main topic will be given 30 min time to solve in the first round.
