Objective :

Encourage participants from all the domains to test their coding skills and help them realize their current level of skill in programming.

Round 1 : Quiz and Aptitude1

  • This round will consist of 20 question which is of 20 points i.e.(Each Question will carry 1 mark ).
  • There will be Cutoff for passing each round.
  • Participant must be able to achieve maximum points ,to secure further position in the Competition.
  • Time limit for these round will be 20 mins .
  • (And will be extended if any technical mismatch

Round 2: programming relay

  • This Round will consist of 40 points , In which Problem statement is given to each Shortlisted team and they have to write code for these.
  • In this round one of the team members will be given time limit of 3-5 minutes for writing half of the code for the given problem.
  • After 3-5 minutes group members will have to switch and other member will continue the previously written code by his/her group member .
  • In the given time limit , we expect a considerable output from that participant's side .
  • Participant must be able to achieve maximum points ,to secure further position in the Competition.

Round 3: Blind Programming

  • This Round will be the Final Stage for the Contest !
  • This Round will consist of 40 points ,In which one of the team member are appearing for these round.
  • In these round participant must given a problem statement and they have to write program BLINDLY , I.e. The Monitor Screen ,will be Turned Off, thus participant will not be able to check the current status of program .
  • Participant will be allowed to turn on the Monitor for only 1 time.
After that if any participant wants to Turn on the Screen , 5 points will be deducted in an Total Score of this particular Round for every instance of Switching on the Screen.

Rules :

  • All the participants must bring their College Id Card.
  • Participants must report half hour before the event start.
  • Participants must bring receipt of the event for verification.
  • Participants should maintain discipline while Attending the event.
  • The decision of Judges will be final.
  • Summation of all the Stages will be considered for the Formation of result.
  • After each round participants are short listed for the next round according to their score.
  • In case of Tie between teams the result will be given according to minimum time taken by the team.
